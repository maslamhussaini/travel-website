Overview

Discover the enchanting island of Bali on this carefully crafted 7-day journey through ancient temples, emerald rice terraces, and pristine beaches. Experience the perfect blend of culture, adventure, and relaxation as you explore one of Southeast Asia's most beloved destinations.

From the artistic heart of Ubud to the stunning cliffside temples of Uluwatu, this tour offers an authentic Balinese experience. Witness breathtaking sunrises, participate in traditional ceremonies, and unwind on world-famous beaches while being guided by local experts who bring the island's rich heritage to life.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day01

Arrival in Bali ‚Äì Welcome to Paradise

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport where your private transfer awaits. Check into your beachfront resort in Seminyak. Evening welcome dinner featuring traditional Balinese cuisine with a cultural dance performance.

Day02

Ubud ‚Äì Art & Culture Immersion

Travel to the cultural heart of Bali. Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest, explore Ubud Royal Palace, and browse the famous Ubud Art Market. Afternoon visit to Tegalalang Rice Terraces for stunning photo opportunities.

Day03

Mount Batur Sunrise Trek

Early morning trek to witness the spectacular sunrise from Mount Batur volcano. Breakfast overlooking the crater lake. Afternoon relaxation at natural hot springs followed by a traditional Balinese cooking class.

Day04

Temple Trail ‚Äì Spiritual Bali

Visit the iconic Tanah Lot sea temple at sunset, followed by the majestic Ulun Danu Beratan temple on the shores of Lake Bratan. Experience a traditional purification ceremony at Tirta Empul holy spring.

Day05

Nusa Penida Island Adventure

Full-day excursion to the stunning Nusa Penida island. Visit Kelingking Beach (T-Rex cliff), Angel's Billabong natural infinity pool, and Broken Beach. Snorkeling with manta rays (seasonal).

Day06

Beach Day & Uluwatu Temple

Morning at leisure ‚Äì enjoy the resort pool or explore Seminyak's boutiques. Late afternoon visit to Uluwatu Temple perched on dramatic cliffs. Watch the mesmerizing Kecak fire dance at sunset.

Day07

Departure ‚Äì Farewell Bali

Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and last-minute shopping. Private transfer to the airport with unforgettable memories of your Bali adventure.
