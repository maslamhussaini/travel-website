FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book directly through our website by selecting your preferred tour and dates, or contact us for personalized assistance. No payment is required until we confirm availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 30 days before departure. Between 15-30 days, a 50% fee applies. Cancellations within 14 days are non-refundable. We recommend travel insurance for added protection.

Are flights included in tour prices?

International flights are not included unless specifically stated. However, we can help arrange flights and often have access to special group rates.

Can you create custom itineraries?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating bespoke journeys tailored to your interests, timeline, and budget. Contact us with your dream destination and we'll make it happen.

What's the typical group size?

Our group tours typically have 8-12 travelers to ensure a personalized experience. Private tours and custom trips can be arranged for any group size.
